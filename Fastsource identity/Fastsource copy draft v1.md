## Notes for the other three product pages

Once Home and AI CRM are approved, Support Agent / Marketing / EduTech each follow this shape:
1. Eyebrow → H1 (the existing one-liner) → subhead that answers the page brief's "visitor's question"
2. 3–4 concrete feature bullets, each an outcome, not an adjective
3. A "who it's for" line — one sentence naming the real persona, including who it's *not* for
4. Proof section — honest interim copy where real proof doesn't exist yet
5. Closing CTA — always "Book a Free Demo," never a variant
