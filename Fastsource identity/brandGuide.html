Voice & tone

Four products need to sound like one company. This is the standard every headline, button, and error message gets written against in Phase 2.

Direct

Lead with the outcome. Say what it does before how it does it.

Concrete

Real numbers and named specifics beat adjectives. A time saved or a step removed, not a feeling.

Warm, not corporate

Sounds like someone on the team, not a legal department. Contractions are fine.

Confident, not hyped

Skip "revolutionary," "seamless," "game-changing." Say what's true and let it stand.
