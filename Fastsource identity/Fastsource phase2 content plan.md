## 2 · Messaging hierarchy

### Company-level promise
**"FastSource builds AI that does the work — not just organizes it."**

Supporting line: *One company, four AI systems — a CRM that works your pipeline, a support agent that answers instantly, marketing that plans and produces campaigns, and EduTech that turns your team into an AI-ready organization.*

This is the thesis every page's hero traces back to. If a headline can't be connected to "does the work, doesn't just organize it," it's drifting off-message.

### Product one-liners
Carried from the original plan, checked against the Phase 1 voice standard (direct, concrete, no hype):

| Product | One-liner | Voice check |
|---|---|---|
| AI CRM | "A CRM that works the pipeline for you — not just stores it." | Passes — already the reference example in the voice guide. |
| AI Support Agent | "Front-line support that answers instantly and escalates smartly." | Passes — concrete verbs, no adjectives doing the work. |
| AI Marketing | "Campaigns planned, produced, and optimized with AI in the loop." | Passes. |
| EduTech | "Turn your team into a skills-driven, AI-ready organization." | Passes, though "AI-ready" is close to becoming a filler term if it shows up on every page — use it once, here, and lean on specifics elsewhere. |

## 3 · Page briefs

Each page's single job, the question the visitor actually has, its one CTA, and the proof it needs to earn belief. No page gets two competing jobs.

### Home
- **Job:** Convince a visitor in under a minute that FastSource is real and credible, then route them to the right product.
- **Visitor's question:** "What does this company actually do, and can I trust it?"
- **CTA:** Book a Free Demo (primary); scroll to product overview (secondary).
- **Proof needed:** client/partner logos, one headline stat, one short testimonial.

### AI Solutions — Overview
- **Job:** Map the three products under this umbrella before the visitor commits to one.
- **Visitor's question:** "Which of these three do I actually need?"
- **CTA:** Book a Free Demo; links into each sub-page.
- **Proof needed:** none critical — this page's job is navigation, not persuasion.

### AI CRM
- **Job:** Convince a sales-ops lead or founder this isn't "just another CRM."
- **Visitor's question:** "How is this different from Salesforce, HubSpot, or Pipedrive?"
- **CTA:** Book a Free Demo.
- **Proof needed:** a concrete metric (e.g. time saved on manual follow-up), one short case study, an integration list.

### AI Support Agent
- **Job:** Show a support/CS lead this handles real ticket volume without losing quality.
- **Visitor's question:** "Will this actually resolve tickets, or just frustrate my customers?"
- **CTA:** Book a Free Demo.
- **Proof needed:** response-time stat, escalation accuracy, a sample conversation transcript.

### AI Marketing
- **Job:** Convince a marketing lead this saves real production time.
- **Visitor's question:** "Does this replace my agency, or work alongside it?"
- **CTA:** Book a Free Demo.
- **Proof needed:** campaign examples, a before/after turnaround-time comparison.

### EduTech
- **Job:** Convince SME leadership this is a credible path to being AI-ready, not just a course.
- **Visitor's question:** "Is this training, consulting, or software — and will my team actually finish it?"
- **CTA:** Book a Free Demo.
- **Proof needed:** completion rate, one case study of a transformed team, a sample curriculum outline.

### About
- **Job:** Build credibility in who's behind this.
- **Visitor's question:** "Who am I actually trusting here?"
- **CTA:** Book a Free Demo (secondary — this page's real job is trust, not conversion).
- **Proof needed:** team bios/photos, the "skills-driven organization" story, partner/community logos.

### Blog / Insights
- **Job:** Signal ongoing expertise; support SEO.
- **Visitor's question:** "Do these people know what they're talking about, consistently?"
- **CTA:** none primary — a soft nudge toward Book a Free Demo at the end of each article is enough.
- **Proof needed:** none — the content itself is the proof.

### Book a Free Demo
- **Job:** Convert, with minimum friction.
- **Visitor's question:** "What happens after I submit this?"
- **CTA:** the form submission itself — copy the confirmation state directly from the Phase 1 voice guide ("You're booked. We'll email a calendar invite in the next few minutes.").
- **Proof needed:** one trust signal near the form (logo strip or testimonial) to counter last-second doubt.

### Contact
- **Job:** Catch non-demo inquiries — support, partnerships, press.
- **Visitor's question:** "How do I reach a human for something that isn't a sales demo?"
- **CTA:** Send message.
- **Proof needed:** none.

### Legal (privacy · terms · cookies)
- **Job:** Compliance and trust signal only. No persuasion, no CTA. Keep this templated — not worth custom voice work.
